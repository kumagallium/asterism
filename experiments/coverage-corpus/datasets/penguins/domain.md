## Domain context

- **Dataset**: Palmer Archipelago penguin morphometrics (palmerpenguins).
- **Purpose**: model individual penguin body measurements for species comparison.
- **Entities**: an individual Penguin specimen observed on an island.
- **Notable columns**: `Species` and `Island` (controlled vocabularies), `Beak Length (mm)`, `Beak Depth (mm)`, `Flipper Length (mm)`, `Body Mass (g)` (units in the column name), `Sex` (MALE/FEMALE).
