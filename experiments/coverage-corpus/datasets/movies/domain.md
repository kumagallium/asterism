## Domain context

- **Dataset**: Film box-office and ratings (vega-datasets `movies`).
- **Purpose**: model each film's financials, release, classification, and review scores.
- **Entities**: a Film (a Work) and its distributor.
- **Notable columns**: `Title`, `US Gross`/`Worldwide Gross`/`Production Budget` (money, USD), `Release Date` (messy "Jun 12 1998" form), `MPAA Rating` and `Major Genre` (controlled vocabularies), `Running Time min`, `IMDB Rating`, `Rotten Tomatoes Rating`.
- **Synonyms**: gross→box office/興行収入, genre→ジャンル, rating→評価/レーティング.
