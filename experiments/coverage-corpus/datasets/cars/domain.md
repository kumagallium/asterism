## Domain context

- **Dataset**: Classic automobile fuel-economy specs (UCI "auto-mpg" style).
- **Purpose**: model each car model's engine/performance specs for comparison and filtering.
- **Entities**: a Car model.
- **Notable columns**: `Name`, `Miles_per_Gallon`, `Cylinders`, `Displacement`, `Horsepower`, `Weight_in_lbs`, `Acceleration`, `Year` (model year), `Origin` (USA/Europe/Japan enum).
- **Synonyms**: mpg→fuel economy/燃費, horsepower→馬力, displacement→排気量.
