## Domain context

- **Dataset**: US airports with location (vega-datasets).
- **Purpose**: model each airport as a place with a code and coordinates.
- **Entities**: an Airport (a Place).
- **Notable columns**: `iata` (3-char code, the natural key), `name`, `city`, `state`, `country`, `latitude`, `longitude`.
- **Synonyms**: iata→airport code/空港コード, lat/long→coordinates/緯度経度.
